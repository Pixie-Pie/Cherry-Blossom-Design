About Me

My name is Cherry Blossom, and I can be your dedicated partner in transforming spaces into artful masterpieces. With a Master of Arts MA in Interior Architecture and Design from the esteemed Academy of Art University in San Francisco, USA, I bring a refined touch to every project. I boast extensive experience in curating remarkable interiors. My portfolio shines with collaborations with high-profile residential and commercial clients - each project is a testament to my creativity.

In recognition of my innovative approach and commitment to excellence, I have been honoured with the prestigious GERMAN DESIGN AWARD SPECIAL MENTION. This award not only underscores my passion for creating captivating spaces but also affirms my dedication to exceeding expectations and setting new industry standards.
